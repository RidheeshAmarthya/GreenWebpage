Leading supplier of fibers, yarns, and fabrics worldwide

Green International Eximp, established in 2000, is a leading India–China textile trading company specializing in premium woven and knitted fabrics, including cotton, polyester, nylon, rayon, and specialty blends.

With offices and production facilities in both countries, we ensure high quality, reliability, and timely global deliveries.

Backed by over two decades of expertise in fabric technology, supply chain management, and international shipping, we are committed to excellence, innovation, and trusted long-term partnerships.

Our Products and Services

Woven

Woven spans across apparel and home furnishing, offering options in greige, blends, and fabrication. Available in solids, yarn-dyed, and all-over prints, our woven range is crafted to meet diverse design and performance needs.

Knits

Knits include warp knits in solid, yarn-dyed, and all-over print options, as well as weft knits available in solid, yarn-dyed, heathers, and all-over prints, delivering flexibility for fashion and furnishing needs.

Yarn

Yarn offerings include cotton, viscose, modal, and polyester, available in raw white, melange, and solid. We also provide all blends with the required certifications to meet diverse industry standards.
